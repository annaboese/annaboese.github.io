About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Anna Boese, and I am currently pursuing a graduate degree at Cornell University specializing in astrodynamics and orbital mechanics. I have a passion for applying mathematical principles and dynamic systems to design and optimize orbital trajectories.

Throughout my academic career, I have developed a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of engineering. My hands-on experience with machines and my proficiency in using advanced software tools enable me to tackle complex engineering problems with confidence.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an avid learner and adventurer. Whether it's hip hop dancing, scuba diving, music production, skydiving, caving, or learning a new language, I embrace opportunities to expand my horizons and make meaningful connections with people from diverse backgrounds.

I believe that my diverse experiences and my enthusiasm for exploring new frontiers contribute to my effectiveness as both an engineer and a leader. I am always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate with others to create innovative solutions.

Feel free to explore my projects and learn more about my journey. Thank you for visiting!
