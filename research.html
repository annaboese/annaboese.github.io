My Focus

The research I have participated in focuses on designing orbits in cislunar space using various models, for example, the circular restricted three-body model or a full force model that defines the Earth as a central body with the Sun and the Moon as perturbers. Other celestial bodies, such as Jupiter, can be easily added to the full force model. A library of initial conditions has been formulated from other research, and these initial conditions can be used in either model to generate an orbit.
